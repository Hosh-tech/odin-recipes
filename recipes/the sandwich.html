The Sandwich

Ciabatta, Chorizo, Halloumi, Peppers, Onions, Tzatziki


Super simple and adaptable sandwich recipe.

Ingredients

  • Ciabatta (ideally homemade subroll, but can be substituted with most shop bought)
  • Chorizo
  • Halloumi
  • Peppers
  • Onions
  • Tzatziki

    • Method

    1. Pre-heat oven for ciabatta bread, 180 - 200 degrees celcius.
    2. Finely chop peppers and onions.
    3. Slice halloumi
    4. Shallow fry till soft and add inclusions such as smoked paprika. Low heat (20mins).
    5. Take off heat and transfer to plate with kitchen roll to soak up oils.
    6. Fry chorizo in same pan till crispy. Low heat.
    7. Transfer to plate with kitchen roll.
    8. Now fry the halloumi in the chorizo oils left in pan, turning frequently till browned.
    9. Place bread into oven till crispy on top and warm throughout.
    10. Slice bread once slightly cooled.
    11. Transfer all ingredients back to pan to warm up for serving.
    12. Spread tzatziki on both open sides of the ciabatta and build your sandwich.
    13. Enjoy!
